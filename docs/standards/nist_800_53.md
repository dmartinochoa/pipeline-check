# NIST SP 800-53 Rev. 5 (CI/CD subset)

- **Version:** Rev. 5
- **URL:** https://csrc.nist.gov/pubs/sp/800/53/r5/upd1/final
- **Source of truth:** `pipeline_check/core/standards/data/nist_800_53.py`
- **Scope:** Controls from the following families whose evidence is
  visible from CI/CD state:

  - **AC** — Access Control (AC-2, AC-3, AC-6)
  - **AU** — Audit and Accountability (AU-2, AU-9, AU-11, AU-12)
  - **CM** — Configuration Management (CM-2, CM-6, CM-7, CM-8)
  - **IA** — Identification and Authentication (IA-5)
  - **RA** — Risk Assessment (RA-5)
  - **SA** — System and Services Acquisition (SA-10, SA-11, SA-15)
  - **SC** — System and Communications Protection (SC-7, SC-8, SC-12,
    SC-13, SC-28)
  - **SI** — System and Information Integrity (SI-2, SI-7)
  - **SR** — Supply Chain Risk Management (SR-3, SR-4, SR-11)

## Not covered

Controls requiring operational / organisational evidence outside the
CI/CD surface — privacy (PT), incident response (IR), personnel (PS),
physical (PE), maintenance (MA), contingency planning (CP), awareness
(AT), and assessment (CA) families — are intentionally omitted.

## Use alongside

When targeting FedRAMP or agency ATO requirements, pair this mapping
with the relevant overlay baseline (Low/Moderate/High) — this module
does not tier controls by impact level.
